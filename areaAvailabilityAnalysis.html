Area Availability Analyzer

Repo

Main Idea

The University of Utah owns a gym facility called the Legacy Gym (also known as the Life Center). The facility has a website that periodically updates showing the amount of people inside the facility, they call this the Area Availability. For the sake of simplificiation, I will call it the Area Availability Website, or AAW for short

The main point of this project is to analyze this number, considering how often it is updated and what can we gather from the given data

This page will show the hours, methodology and results from this project, as well as some future predictions of what this could entail

Methodology

My methodology was simple, but there wasa lot I had to learn to make it work as expected:

  1. Start the script by opening the AAW
  2. Look at the current availability, and save that to a variable
  3. Establish a connection with some sort of database and store that variable number
  4. Close the website
  5. Repeat after x amount of time
